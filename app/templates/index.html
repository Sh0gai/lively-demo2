Jones County, Georgia

Heart of Middle Georgia

Discover the charm and heritage of Jones County, Georgia - a beautiful community rich in history, natural beauty, and Southern hospitality.

Located in the heart of Middle Georgia, Jones County offers small-town charm with easy access to major cities. Founded in 1807, our county features historic sites, beautiful landscapes, and a thriving community that welcomes visitors and residents alike.

Natural Beauty

Jones County features beautiful forests, scenic rivers, and abundant wildlife, perfect for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Rich History

With historic antebellum homes, Civil War sites, and generations of Southern heritage, Jones County preserves Georgia's storied past.
